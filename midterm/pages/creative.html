Snoopy!



Snoopy is a beagle that appears in the comic strip Peanuts by Charles M. Schulz. Snoopy is a fantastic dog, with soaring ambitions (although I'm slightly biased). Snoopy would like to be an aviator, an author, and overall the best in the world. Snoopy also likes to imagine himself as a cool college student, one that he calls, unsurprisingly, "Joe Cool." Of the many characters featured in the Charlie Brown comics, Snoopy is my all-time favorite. His personality, with all its strengths and flaws, is very endearing.
